HĀV Deliverable 03

Landing Page

A conversion-focused page structure for introducing HĀV clearly, building trust, and guiding families or professionals toward the next step.

Hero

Headline

Understand behavior. Take informed action. Track meaningful progress.

Subheadline

HĀV gives families and professionals a shared way to identify patterns, choose next steps, and support progress over time.

Problem

When behavior feels hard to understand, families and professionals can lose time trying to piece together what happened, what changed, and what to do next.

Solution

Shared Insight

Bring observations and context into one clearer view.

Practical Tools

Support better next steps without adding unnecessary complexity.

Visible Progress

Track what is changing and make improvement easier to see.

How It Works

1. Observe

Record what is happening in real situations.

2. Understand

Identify patterns, triggers, needs, and useful context.

3. Act

Use informed support strategies and track outcomes.

Support works better when everyone can see the same picture.

Use HĀV to bring clarity, coordination, and progress into behavioral support.
