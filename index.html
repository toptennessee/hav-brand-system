Keep the name. Clarify the system.

This refinement keeps HĀV's original behavioral-support center while giving it a more credible, warmer, and more structured identity.

1

Understand behavior

The brand begins with clarity because caregivers and professionals need a shared view of what is happening before they can act well.

2

Take informed action

The platform should feel practical, not abstract. The identity supports decisions, routines, intervention tools, and next steps.

3

Track real progress

Progress is the proof layer. The visual system uses charts, sequence, and structure to reinforce measurable improvement over time.

Messaging framework

The words should move in the same sequence as the product experience: clarity, action, progress.

Use

Understand behavior with more clarity.
Turn observations into practical next steps.
Support families and professionals working together.
Track progress over time.

Avoid

Overpromising stress reduction.
Making the brand feel like a generic wellness app.
Using language that sounds too clinical for families.
Replacing behavior support with vague care language.

Psychology behind the choices

This gives you the explanation layer: not just what changed, but why it works.

Deep navy

Creates trust and authority. It keeps the brand credible for clinicians, educators, and families making serious decisions.

Teal

Signals clarity and movement. It is healthcare-adjacent without feeling cold, making it useful for action states and insight moments.

Sage

Softens the clinical structure. It introduces regulation, care, and steadiness without turning the brand into a lifestyle/wellness identity.

Coral

Adds human warmth and emotional reward. It should appear sparingly for milestones, encouragement, and moments of progress.

Rounded cards

Reduce visual threat and cognitive load. The interface should feel organized, calm, and usable under stress.

Three-step message

Understand → Act → Progress mirrors the user journey. It gives the brand a repeatable structure instead of a collection of disconnected claims.
