Brand Kit Logo direction, colors, typography, voice, tone, and usage standards. Included
Positioning

HĀV is a behavioral support platform that helps families and professionals understand behavior, take informed action, and track meaningful progress together.

Brand Feel

Clear enough for professionals. Warm enough for families. Structured enough for behavior tracking.

Logo Direction

Use a simple HĀV wordmark with a strong H mark. Avoid unnecessary symbolism that may distort the identity or create unintended associations.

Typography

Use Inter or a similarly clean digital sans-serif. Headlines should be bold, clear, and structured. Body copy should remain direct and easy to scan.

Usage Rules
  • Keep HĀV as the name.
  • Lead with behavioral support, not generic wellness.
  • Use coral sparingly for milestones, approvals, and progress moments.
  • Prioritize clarity over decoration.
  • Protect credibility for professional and family audiences.
