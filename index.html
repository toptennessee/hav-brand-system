Brand Kit.

Logo direction, visual system, voice, tone, and usage rules for a clearer HĀV identity.

Core Brand Direction

HĀV is a behavioral support platform that helps families and professionals understand behavior, take informed action, and track meaningful progress together.

The brand should feel clear enough for professionals, warm enough for families, and structured enough for behavior tracking.

```

Logo Direction

Use a simple HĀV wordmark with a strong H mark. Avoid extra symbolism that may distort the identity or create unintended associations.

The brand should gain strength through clarity, spacing, color, hierarchy, and language rather than decorative logo complexity.

```

Color System

Each color has a specific role. Use coral sparingly.

Usage Rules

These rules prevent the brand from drifting away from its actual purpose.

Keep HĀV

The refinement strengthens the existing name instead of renaming the platform.

```

Lead With Behavioral Support

Do not reposition HĀV as generic wellness, general family care, or vague caregiver navigation.

Use Plain Language

Clinical terms are useful only when they improve clarity. Avoid jargon where families need direct understanding.

Protect Credibility

The brand should feel polished enough for professionals and approachable enough for families.

```
