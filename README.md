# HĀV

Behavior Platform for Families Navigating Autism Care

---

## Overview

HĀV is a caregiver-centered platform designed to help families navigate the realities of autism support, intervention, services, and daily caregiving.

The platform is built around a simple premise:

Caregivers often know something needs attention.

They do not always know what happens next.

HĀV provides structure, guidance, resources, and support that help families move from uncertainty toward action.

---

## Problem

Families caring for children with autism often experience:

- High levels of stress
- Service fragmentation
- Scheduling complexity
- Financial pressure
- Social isolation
- Information overload
- Difficulty coordinating care

Research indicates that successful intervention can improve child outcomes while caregiver stress often remains elevated.

This reveals an important gap:

Improving behavior does not automatically improve navigation.

---

## Position

HĀV is not solely an intervention platform.

HĀV is a navigation platform.

The objective is to help caregivers understand:

- What is happening
- What matters most
- What to do next

---

## Core Principles

### Clarity

Reduce uncertainty.

### Guidance

Provide actionable next steps.

### Coordination

Connect people, services, and information.

### Progress

Support long-term family outcomes.
