# Brand Strategy

## Position

HĀV is a behavioral support platform designed to help families understand behavior, take informed action, and measure progress over time.

---

## Core Problem

Families often experience uncertainty when navigating behavioral challenges.

They need:

- Clarity
- Guidance
- Collaboration
- Progress

---

## Strategic Role

HĀV bridges the gap between understanding behavior and acting effectively.

---

## Value

Understanding creates confidence.

Confidence creates action.

Action creates progress.
