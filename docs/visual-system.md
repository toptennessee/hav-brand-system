# Visual System

## Psychological Goals

The visual identity should communicate:

- Trust
- Stability
- Clarity
- Support
- Progress

---

## Emotional Outcome

Families should feel:

"I understand what is happening."

rather than:

"I am overwhelmed."

---

## Color Psychology

### Navy

Trust
Professionalism
Reliability

### Teal

Clarity
Insight
Action

### Sage

Support
Growth
Balance

### Coral

Encouragement
Progress
Positive reinforcement

---

## Design Principle

Reduce uncertainty.

Increase confidence.
