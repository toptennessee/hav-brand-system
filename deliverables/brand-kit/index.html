HĀV Deliverable 01

Brand Kit

The HĀV brand kit defines the visual and verbal standards for a behavioral support platform built around clarity, informed action, shared support, and meaningful progress.

Core Brand Direction

Positioning

HĀV is a behavioral support platform that helps families and professionals understand behavior, take informed action, and track meaningful progress together.

Brand Feel

Clear enough for professionals. Warm enough for families. Structured enough for behavior tracking.

Message Sequence

Understand behavior. Take informed action. Track meaningful progress.

Logo System

H
HĀV

Simple mark. Strong name. No extra symbolism unless it improves clarity.

Color Palette

Typography

Primary Typeface

Inter is recommended for its readability, digital clarity, and clean professional structure.

Headlines

Use bold, concise headlines with strong hierarchy. Avoid decorative or overly soft typefaces.

Body Copy

Use plain language. Keep sentences direct. Clinical language should only be used when it adds clarity.

Voice & Tone

Use

Clear, steady, practical, supportive, direct, professional, human.

Avoid

Overly clinical, overly cozy, vague wellness language, emotional overpromising, generic app language.

Best Tone

Clinical clarity plus family support.

Usage Rules

Keep HĀV as the name. The refinement strengthens the existing identity instead of renaming the platform.
Lead with behavioral support. Avoid repositioning HĀV as only caregiver navigation or general family wellness.
Use coral sparingly. Coral works best for milestones, approvals, progress states, and warmth accents.
Prioritize clarity. Every design choice should make the platform easier to understand, trust, or use.
Protect credibility. The brand should feel polished enough for professionals and approachable enough for families.
